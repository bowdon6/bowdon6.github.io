Bowdon v Oxton 6 Match Report

Nov 12th, 2012 | 2012, Dave Brooks, league | Comments

I get the feeling that Mr Brooks isn’t a 100% happy with Mr Prosser…

After the abysmal week in Llandudno the team rallied to the cause to offer el captain the choice of 17 for this week’s visit of Oxton. Somehow the losing team all managed to retain their places in the squad with frank “who?” fallon making himself available, only to then hurt his hand? (do you think we haven’t noticed the new girlfriend?). Not that it mattered as he was going to the 7’s anyway.

The day began with the captain showing he can pass while warming up with his son (the sad thing is he’s better than most of us and he’s only 7 – AB), however this appears to only be a training drill. We “welcomed” the Scousers to a warm sunny day at Alti Boys with them delaying the start as the captain felt it wasn’t fair to make them play with only 8? (SCOUSERS Braithers, SCOUSERS!!!)

Bowdon started off sloppy allowing the opposition to play some nice hockey for the first 30 seconds until Bretton popped up on the right hand side a drilled in a cross which MANTERS converted. And so it began….

the crowd are somewhat surprised by Kyle’s goal…

The best 6th Team in Bowdon then started to live up to why they are the best with another 2 goal in quick succession from Manters and….wait for it…. KYLE HATCH!!!!!! Some sloppy and selfish play resulted in a 10man Bowdon side making it to half time with a clean sheet and having only scored 3! (You don’t count as playing if you don’t touch the ball Dave).

The second half began with Bowdon realising there is no “I” in team (unfortunately this message did not convey to the captain who just wanted to score and not pass to anyone). Brooks started on the bench and thought his ears were going as the supported, yes supporters, were commentating on DALE’S SKILL???

This next bit may take reading 2 or 3 times so I will put it in capitals. MANTERS THEN GOT US GOING WITH ANOTHER 2 GOALS!!!! Before Oxtons defender then decided to chip his own keeper with what was to be oxtons only shot on goal in the game. Mosedale added another for the mighty 6th team and then Brooks slotted home from close range with a ball going so slowly even Dave may have saved it. Brooks nearly doubled his tally until producing the best air shot you will see all season.

Artists impression of Prosser’s goal Then comes the most disturbing and quite frankly unacceptable bit of camaraderie I have ever seen. It was so bad it is something I would only think of happening at Lymm and Timperley! Brooks shot at goal with a thunder of a strike only for it to hit the Oxton keeper (who must have been in some relation to the giants!) but with a lucky rebound back out to him, beat the keeper at the second attempt with a sublime drag flick which was just about to cross the line at chest height and seal off a perfect day. AND THEN… RICHARD “the honorary Scouser” PROSSER stuck a stick out to steal it for himself. Maybe he did not get the code of conduct when he joined the club but this is NOT TIMPERLEY anymore!

However a 9-0 win makes us all happy and we travel to Wilmslow next week with hope of another emphatic victory and a bit more team spirit (Prosser and Braithers)!

Unfortunately I cannot find anything this week to bully dale about, however with all his “skill” he still managed not to score and the man of the match evaded him again. With Mosedale goal and 2 assists and all round tireless performance gifting him the honour!
