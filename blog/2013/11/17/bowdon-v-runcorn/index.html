Bowdon v Runcorn

Nov 17th, 2013 | 2013, Gareth Perry, league | Comments

Better late than never. This week’s report is from Mr G. Perry

Bowdon visited one of the most beautiful hockey grounds in West Warrington for their game against Runcorn. Dale Jago was very fortunate as it’s was a home game in reality. The newly self appointed fascist dictator, Herr Mr I’m Not Sure Where I’m From Hatch gave one of the most unrousing team talks ever, pretty much destroying any morale which we had. As a direct result of such an uninspiring team talk we went one nil down. Thankfully Perry was on hand to level with a Gareth Bale-esque 59 yard weaving run and finish. Runcorn retook the lead, despite Freds dodgy touch the goal was still blamed on the captains poor prematch chat. At 2:1 Bowdon at half time the new skipper laid into his troops and got the reaction which his words commanded. A stunning second half display insued with 3 fine goals unanswered by the home team. The 4:2 final score may have flattered to deceive but in reality the discerning hockey brain knew that Bowdon had simply worn down the opposition to submission. The conclusion to the game was kindly summed up by words from Alan Hansen, a good team wins even when it is playing badly. Long may this fine run of bad form continue!
