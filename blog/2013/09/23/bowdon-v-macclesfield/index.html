Bowdon v Macclesfield

Sep 23rd, 2013 | 2013, Gareth Perry, league | Comments

As promised, I have limited the report to only 3 words for each player. In short, the 6th team launched their 2013-14 league campaign in the fashion which their supporters have become accustomed to, beating Macclesfield 11-0, top go to the top of the league.

The following performers all played their part:

  • Harry the Cat - watched as spectator
  • Nick West - kept Harry awake
  • Francis J. Fallon (c) - lead by example
  • Chris Gawne - got nosebleed attacking
  • Ben Fallon - over-shadowed by brother
  • Karl Bailey - Hull defeated Newcastle
  • Neil Breton - chief of assists
  • Dale Jago - mis-hit short corner
  • Andrew Braithwaite - conceded needless PC
  • Kyle Hatch - got cramp mincing
  • Gareth Perry - bankrupt after goalscoring

We also had a twelfth member but I can’t remember who (apologies no offence meant)? - AB: it was young Ben, who had an excellent game

Many thanks to Martin Ferrett and Tony Davies for umpiring.

AB: Frank has since found out that the Macc keeper broke his leg. I’m sure you’ll all join me in wishing him a speedy recovery

Top of the league!!!
